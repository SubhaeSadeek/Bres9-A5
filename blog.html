The situation in Noakhali flood has been improving for the last 24 hours.

The situation in Noakhalki is imporoving since yesterday. Water is receding in fe w areas but all area are still under water in Noakhali. We are managing 40 more boats to include in our rescue operation. Water is not incresing in most of the area and current is not too strong like last week. It seems, rescue work are now less risky and it is an opprtunity to speed up rescue operation. Children and elderly are specially in denger and it is estimated that we only rescued merely 40% of the flood victim. Begumganj and Maijdee upazila are most vulnerable of all.


We are forming a coalition with several prominent volunteer organization of Noakhali. We want to speed up rescue operation and in perrarale we are engaging half of our resources to post flood rehabilitation programme. The major rehabilitation programme we are planning is to ensure waterborne diseases are on check, tubewell and sanitary toilets are restored and direct help to rebuild house that are destroyed. All the donations we get are promised to spend on most essential items and in most effective manners. We croud-source many activities that require physical labor. People are volunteering in neighboring disticts we made temporary working stations.
